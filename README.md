📊 Customer Service Analytics Dashboard

Project Summary
This project demonstrates my ability to transform raw customer data into actionable business insights using Power BI.
The dashboard provides a clear view of customer behavior, revenue drivers, and service usage patterns, enabling stakeholders to make informed decisions on retention, pricing, and support strategies.

---
 Business Problem
Organizations often struggle to:
* Understand what drives customer revenue
* Identify high-value customer segments
* Analyze the impact of support services on revenue
* Track customer lifecycle and tenure
* 
This dashboard addresses these gaps through interactive and data-driven visualization.

---
Key Outcomes & Insights

* Revenue Concentration: Fiber optic customers contribute the largest share of total revenue
* Customer Value: Long-term contracts generate significantly higher total charges
* Support Impact: Customers without tech support show higher monthly charges (potential upsell opportunity)
* Customer Distribution:Revenue contribution is nearly evenly split across genders
* Retention Indicator: Customers with longer tenure contribute more to total revenue

---
📊 Dashboard Capabilities
Interactive filtering by:
  * Gender
  * Tech support
  * Contract type
* Revenue breakdown by service type
* Customer segmentation analysis
* Trend analysis for tenure and dependency
