Customer Service Analytics Dashboard
 Project Summary
This project demonstrates my ability to transform raw customer data into actionable business insights using Power BI.
The dashboard provides a clear view of customer behavior, revenue drivers, and service usage patterns, enabling stakeholders to make informed decisions on retention, pricing, and support strategies.

Business Problem:
Organizations often struggle to:
*Understand what drives customer revenue
*Identify high-value customer segments
*Analyze the impact of support services on revenue
*Track customer lifecycle and tenure
This dashboard addresses these gaps through interactive and data-driven visualization.


 Features
* Total customer overview
* Monthly and total revenue tracking
* Customer segmentation by:
  * Gender
  * Payment method
  * Internet service type
  * Contract type
* Tech support analysis
* Tenure and dependency insights

 📈 Key Metrics
Total Customers: 7,043

 📊 Dashboard Insights

* Gender distribution of monthly charges is nearly balanced
* Fiber optic customers contribute the highest revenue
* Customers without tech support generate higher charges
* Long-term contracts show higher total charges
